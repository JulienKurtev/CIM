About Us

The CIM Academy is CIM's on-line learning portal where CIM conference and other events recorded presentations are made available to members of CIM and the Minerals community at large. These presentations. combining audio,video and PowerPoint allow the community to access content otherwise only available to in-person conference attendees. The platform alows users to search for content by event, by contributor,by primary or seconary topic as well as by keywords.
